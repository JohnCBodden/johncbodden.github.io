About Me

Having spent the past 25+ years within the Information Technology field I have garnered diverse and extensive experience throughout my professional career, including, and not limited to; security, networking, troubleshooting, installations and web development, and all the matters related to the aforementioned.

As the IT Industry continues to rapidly evolve and the absolute need for organizations to remain highly defended against potential threats by remaining many steps ahead of potential cyber-attacks; I am confident that my training and qualification as a ‘Certified Penetration Testing Engineer’ (“Ethical Hacker”) can prove to be an asset to any firm. This coveted designation has instilled the valuable ability to anticipate potential threats and plots of a cyber-criminal as well as knowledge and training with the various tools utilized to initiate attacks. By knowing and understanding the thought patterns and “virtual arsenal” used by cyber-criminals, I am best-placed to ensure that processes are implemented before threats emerge.

During my professional career I have overseen the network security of local eCommerce Gateways in which my duties included ensuring the safety and security of card data of thousands of customers. Over the decade which I provided this service there was not a single breach or interception of customer data.
