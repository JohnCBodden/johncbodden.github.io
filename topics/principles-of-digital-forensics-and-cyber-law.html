Activity 1: What is Law?
While reading the Gearty (2019) article, keep in mind the following question: what is the point that Gearty is trying to make?

Part 1
The author Gearty (2019) discusses the historical and ongoing challenge that human societies face in establishing systems of control and governance. Gearty (2019) emphasises the need for humans to find ways to control themselves for the benefit of the larger community. Gearty (2019) explores the question of how to resolve disputes and maintain order without descending into chaos. Gearty (2019) mentions different forms of authority that societies have historically relied on, such as the rule of monarchs, the influence of priests, and the adherence to custom and tradition. The underlying theme is the perennial quest for a system of governance that ensures social order and harmony.
